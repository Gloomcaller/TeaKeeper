About TeaKeeper

TeaKeeper is a simple web application for tracking your tea drinking habits.

How to Use:

1. View Your Teas

Go to Teas to see all your recorded teas. You can edit or delete entries.

2. Add New Teas

Use the Import/Export page to:

  • Upload a CSV file with your tea data
  • Download your current tea list as CSV
  • Reset to sample data

3. View Calendar

The Calendar shows when you drank tea. Days are color-coded:

  • No tea
  • 1 tea
  • 2 teas
  • 3+ teas

4. Check Statistics

Visit Statistics for insights:

  • Most popular brands
  • Monthly tea consumption
  • Flavor preferences

A. CSV Format

Your CSV file should have this format:

            drink_date,brand,tea_name,flavor
            2025-01-15,Fructus,Earl Grey,Bergamot
            2025-01-16,dmBio,Peppermint,Mint
